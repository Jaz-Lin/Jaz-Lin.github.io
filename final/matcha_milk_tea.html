Matcha Milk Tea

A popular drink with its vibrant green and smooth texture. A good obsession to have.

Description

The history of matcha itself can be traced back to the Tang Dynasty in China - it later spread to Japan when the seeds of the plant was grown around Kyoto. There's a long history of its usage in tea ceremonies as well as list of health benefits that come from the consumption of matcha. Matcha milk tea is a beverage that is made from the mixture of matcha and milk and can be served either hot or iced.
