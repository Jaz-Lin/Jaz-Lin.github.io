Taro Milk Tea

Delicious and refreshing with a nice, purple color. Better than your typical vanilla milkshake and with a rich nutty flavor.

Description

Originating from Taiwan in the 1980s, it gained traction throughout Asia. The base of the beverage is made up of with some kind of tea mixed with milk. Taro is later added in. The purple coloring of the the drink comes from the taro, a starchy and fibrous plant whose root and stems are broken down for consumption. Its fibrous content also thickens the drink.
